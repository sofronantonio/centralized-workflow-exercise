# Guacamole recipe


## Ingredients

- 2 avocados (hass)
- 1 chili (jalapenos) (in case of allergy replace with 2 tsp paprika)
- 1 lime
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 red onion
- 1 Tbsp fresh cilantro


## Instructions

- chop avocados
- chop onion
- chop chili
- squeeze lime
- add salt
- and mix well
- enjoy!
